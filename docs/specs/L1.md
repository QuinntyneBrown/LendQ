# LendQ — L1 High-Level Requirements

## L1-1: User Authentication & Login
The system shall provide a login screen allowing users to authenticate with credentials (email/username and password), including sign-up, forgot-password, and session management flows.

## L1-2: User Management & RBAC
The system shall provide administrative screens for managing users, roles, and permissions. Roles include at minimum: Admin, Creditor, and Borrower. A single user may hold multiple roles simultaneously (e.g., be both a creditor and a borrower).

## L1-3: Loan Management
The system shall allow creditors to create, view, edit, and manage loans. Each loan includes a principal amount, description, borrower, repayment terms (weekly, monthly, custom), interest rate (optional), and status. Creditors can update any field on their loans. Borrowers can view their loans and update everything except the principal amount.

## L1-4: Payment Tracking & Scheduling
The system shall track all payments against a loan, show next payment due, payment history, and outstanding balance. Borrowers can reschedule payments, pause payments, or make lump-sum payments. Creditors can adjust any payment details. The system shall display changed/rescheduled payments clearly.

## L1-5: Dashboard & Overview
The system shall provide a dashboard showing a summary of all lending activity for the logged-in user — money lent out, money owed, upcoming payments, overdue payments, and total outstanding balances across all loans (as both creditor and borrower).

## L1-6: Notifications & Alerts
The system shall provide in-app notifications for payment due dates, overdue payments, payment received confirmations, loan modifications, and schedule changes. Notification dialogs and toast messages shall be designed.

## L1-7: Responsive Design
All screens shall be designed with responsive layouts supporting mobile (375px), tablet (768px), and desktop (1280px+) viewports. All buttons, dialogs, notifications, forms, and navigation shall adapt to each breakpoint.
